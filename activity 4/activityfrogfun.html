how to make a sandwich

disclaimer: please note that frogs do not possess the necessary tools (thumbs, for starters) to make sandwiches. but if a frog could suddenly make a sandwich, they would surely make it like this.

  1. get two small pieces of bread, or perhaps two leaves.
  2. catch a fly with your tongue. easy.
  3. put the fly in between the two pieces of bread/leaves
  4. make sure the fly is dead and smashed, so the fly doesn't manage to get way and ruin your day.
  5. garnish or fancy topings are unnecessary. don't bother.
  6. enjoy your sandwich using your new opposable thumbs.
