how to make a sandwich

you will need the following:

  • a knife (optional)
  • a plate
  • sliced bread
  • deli cut meat of your choice
  • cheese slices of your choice
  • any other toppings or sauces you want (lettuce, bacon, etc)

directions:

  1. place 2 bread slices on your plate.
  2. if you want to use sauce(s), use a knife to spread the sauce on one or both slices of bread. only smear on one side of each slice.
  3. lay the cheese slices on one slice of bread.
  4. layer the deli meat onto the cheese.
  5. add other toppings, such as bacon, by putting them on the deli meat.
  6. place the other slice of bread on top of this slice, making sure to put the sauce side facing the other slice.
  7. (optional) take a knife and cut your sandwich in half, in quarters, or diagonally.
